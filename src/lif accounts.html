Whats a Lif Account?

A Lif Account is and account you create with Lif Platforms that allows you to log into all of our products and services with just one account.

Features

One Account Sign In

Lif accounts allow you to use one account to sign into all of Lif Platforms products and services.

Built-In Security

Lif Accounts have been built from the ground up to be safe, secure, and protect the data of their owners.

Privacy at The Forefront

Lif Accounts have been designed to protect the privacy of their owners. They don't collect any data that is not needed for the service to function.
